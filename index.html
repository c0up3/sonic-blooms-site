Sonic Blooms is a fictional alternative music project about masking, anxiety, damaged intimacy, static, machinery, and the strange beauty of people who can only tell the truth through sound.

Explore the music Spotify

Signal / damage / devotion

Luca is the raw human signal. Seraphine is the cracked machine angel.

Their story moves through panic rooms, broken stages, corrupted halos, failed confession, and music as the only language that survives when speech collapses.

The emotional engine

Not clean romance. Not clean fear. Something wired between them.

Luca Ashborne is anxious, overloaded, masked, and painfully human. Seraphine Voss is porcelain, brass, rust, broken halo, artificial heart, and real agency. They fail at ordinary conversation, but they hear each other in static, harmonies, silence, and production.

I brought the wrong face again. The real one screams, but the mask sings better. Every song already had your name in it.
